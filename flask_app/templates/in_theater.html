The shining

Coming in: came long ago

"The Shining" is a psychological horror novel by Stephen King, later adapted into a classic film directed by Stanley Kubrick. It tells the story of a writer, Jack Torrance, who takes a winter caretaker job at the haunted Overlook Hotel. Isolation and supernatural forces drive him to madness, endangering his family.
